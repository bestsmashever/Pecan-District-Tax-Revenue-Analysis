Executive Summary

This mixed-use development project represents a $215M investment generating substantial long-term tax revenue for the city and Pflugerville ISD. The development includes 311 multifamily units, 143 townhomes, 53,776 sq ft of retail space, and 275,366 sq ft of office space, with groundbreaking scheduled for 2027 and delivery in 2029.
